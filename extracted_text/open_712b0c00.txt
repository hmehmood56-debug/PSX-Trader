Technical Analysis       
Strategy: The Bulls continued their journey on Wednesday & the benchmark Kse-100 index 
concluded the session at a level of 42,142.71 with a gain of 219.25 points. A bullish formation of 
higher high and higher low was registered on daily chart and closed above the upper level of the 
Bollinger band, which indicates a continuation of bullish momentum. Immediate continuation of 
current bullish trend is targeting 42,248 – 42,375 area with immediate support placed in at 41,500. A 
break above 42,375 would eye 42,471 – 42,582 points. Immediate supports stands in b/w 42,040 – 
41,965. Relapse under 41,965 would instill weakness to 41,850 – 41,780 points. Since the benchmark 
index is about to enter a congestion area in between 42,300 – 42,600, it is likely to remain volatile. 
We believe that there is still rising power in the index, which can help bulls gain further territory 
towards 42,248– 42,375 level mark.
