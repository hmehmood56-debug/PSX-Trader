Technical Analysis       
Strategy: Local market witnessed a volatile session on Monday with the Kse-100 index oscillating 
between 42,029 points intra-day high (up by 124 points) and 41,715 points intra-day low level (down 
by 188 points) and finally the index closed at a level of 41,782.92 with a decline of 121.28 points. The 
benchmark Kse-100 finally concluded the session at a level of 41,782.92 with a net decline of 121.28 
points. This scenario now unveils the index levels to be in between 41,718 – 41,648 as the next 
possible level to be seen. The same area also acts as the immediate support for now.  In case, the 
aforementioned support fails to hold, further price attrition towards 41,540 – 41,452 and later 41,300 
cannot be ruled out. On the other side any upside move from here would, face strong resistance in 
b/w 41,852 – 41,920 and strong move above 41,920 would helps the Bulls to continue the rally 
towards 42,006 – 42,140.
