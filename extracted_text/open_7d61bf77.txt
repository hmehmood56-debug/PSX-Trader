Technical Analysis       
Strategy: Bulls attempted to show some strength earlier in the session however, they failed to 
muster enough traction and succumbed to nervousness. The benchmark Kse-100 finally concluded 
the session at a level of 41,538.72 with a net decline of 244.20 points. This scenario now unveils the 
index levels to be in between 41,464 – 41,375 as the next possible level to be seen. The same area 
also acts as the immediate support for now.  In case, the aforementioned support fails to hold, further 
price attrition towards 41,254 – 41,155 and later 41,000 cannot be ruled out. On the other hand, since 
the benchmark index is trading below its short term moving averages any upside move is likely to 
face strong resistance from the moving averages currently stalling in the band of 41,640 – 41,900.
