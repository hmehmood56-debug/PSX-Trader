 Technical Analysis       
Strategy:  The index traded inside the previous candle registered a higher low & lower high while 
concluded the session with positive number. The current chart pattern indicates that another attempt 
to penetrate through the newly formed resistive range of 41,975 – 42,040 could be seen. Should the 
market be able to penetrate through the mentioned ceiling level, it would hint a test of another & major 
resistance area, which is defined in b/w 42,120 – 42,224 and later on 42,350 would be on card. On 
the other hand, risk of downside will be triggered on close below the supportive range of 41,817 – 
41,718. Failure to hold 41,718 would instill liquidation risk to 41,660 – 41,539 and later 41,430 – 
41,270 would be on cards.
