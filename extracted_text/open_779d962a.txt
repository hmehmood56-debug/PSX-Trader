From Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
June 13, 2023 
 
• A mixed session was recorded at the PSX today. The market opened in the green after the SBP kept the interest rate unchanged in the MPC meeting held yesterday. Although 
later stocks tumbled as investors opted to book profits at the end of the day as the index ended in the red shedding net 246.77 points. Investors participation remained sideways 
as the 3rd tier stocks continued to dominate the volume board. 
• The index closed at 41,538.72 down by 244.20pts (-0.58% DoD). Sectors contributing to the performance include Cement (-73.4pts), Technology & Communication (-52.0pts), 
Fertilizer (-42.4pts), Chemical (-33.3pts) and Food & Personal Care Products (-23.9pts).  
• Volumes decreased from 179.1mn shares to 144.2mn shares (-19.5% DoD). The average traded value also decreased by 26.3% to USD 14.3mn as against USD 19.3mn. 
• Stocks that contributed significantly to the volumes were HASCOL, TPLP, WTL, BOP and MDTL.
