From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
December 31, 2024 
• 
Year end selling kept the KSE-100 below the recent highs  
• 
49 shares rose while 50 fell with PPL PA (+2.3%), UBL PA (+1.03%) and HBL PA (+1.7%) contributed the most 
to index gains  
• 
FFC PA (-1.51%), MCB PA (-2.1%) and BAFL PA (-2.1%) were the biggest index drags  
• 
According to media reports, the government has approved the sale of a 15% stake in the Reko Diq Project to 
Saudi Arabia for USD 540mn (~PKR 150bn). Saudi Arabia is expected to make the payment in two phases: USD 
330mn in the Phase-I and the remaining USD 210mn in the Phase-II. Federal Government’s stake is held through 
OGDC, PPL, and GHPL, with each holding an 8.33% stake. In the event of the sale of stake, OGDC and PPL are 
expected to book gain of investment of PKR 2.56/share and PKR 4.12/share, respectively. Additionally, the cash 
flow impact is projected at PKR 11.63/share for OGDC and PKR 18.39/share for PPL. 
• 
The KSE-100 closed out the year with a gain of +88% (USD) to rank #2 globally  
• 
Heading into the new year the KSE-100 averages +4% in January over the last 10-yrs  
•
