From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
June 27, 2025 
• 
Very strong week for Pakistan with the KSE-100 +3.6% WoW to close above 124k 
• 
On Friday the KSE-100 gained +1.91% with FFC PA (+2.23%), LUCK PA (+4.28%) and 
MEBL PA (+2.98%) contributing the most to index gains  
• 
PSEL PA (-8.3%), NATF PA (-1.76%), ISL PA (-0.8%) were the biggest index drags  
• 
PM Sharif and US Secretary of State Marco Rubio spoke and agreed to continue working 
closely to strengthen bilateral relations, particularly through enhanced trade 
• 
GoP has received commercial loans of USD 3.1bn and multilateral loans of more than USD 
500mn, which will be reflected in total reserves announcement later today 
• 
POL PA (+6.06%) and MOL find deposits from Makori Deep-03 in KP which will produce 
22mcf/d gas and 2,112 barrels/day oil 
• 
For the coming week support is anticipated around 122k and 127k will be the upside draw
