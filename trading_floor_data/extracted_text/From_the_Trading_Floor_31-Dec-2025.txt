From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
December 31, 2025 
• The KSE-100 closed the year with new all time highs being made with touched 175.2k to post a gain of 
+51.5% in CY25 
• 52 shares rose while 46 fell with BAHL PA (+1.22%), JVDC PA (+10.0%) and EFERT PA (+1.05%) 
contributing the most to index gains  
• LUCK PA (-2.52%), FFC PA (-0.95%) and NBP PA (-1.61%) were the biggest index drags  
• DGKC PA (-3.66%) has established a letter of credit to set up largest single clinker line at Dera Ghazi Khan 
site, the production line will have a capacity of 11,000 tons clinker/day and will be the biggest in the country 
• Another solid year concludes for the KSE-100 and the outlook remains positive heading into January
