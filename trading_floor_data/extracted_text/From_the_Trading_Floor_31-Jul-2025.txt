From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
July 31, 2025 
• 
Supply above 140k continues to cap gains for the KSE-100 
• 
60 shares rose while 39 fell with SYS PA (+7.5%), OGDC PA (+3.66%), POL PA (+5.02%) contributed the 
most to index gains  
• 
FFC PA (-1.41%), BAFL PA (-2.1%) and EFERT PA (-0.73%) were the biggest index drags  
• 
Oil names were all well bid and saw the most value traded following comments from President Trump that 
his administration has struck a deal with Pakistan in which Washington will work with Islamabad in 
developing the South Asian nation’s oil reserves. There was no update on tariffs which the market continues 
to eagerly wait for  
• 
BAFL PA (-2.33%) announced 1HCY25 EPS: 9.55, -31% YoY and DPS 5.0  
• 
HBL PA (-0.58%) announced 1HCY25 EPS: 23.44, +16% YoY and DPS 9.0 
• 
The strong gap at the start of the session sees near term support rise to 138.6k-139.4k and heading into the 
last session of the week the KSE-100 is currently +0.13% WoW with the 140.5k upside draw still open
