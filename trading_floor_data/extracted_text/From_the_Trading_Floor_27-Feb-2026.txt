From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
February 27, 2026 
• Consolidation following yesterday’s surge and escalating tensions with Afghanistan to take declines to -2.9% 
WoW and -8.75% MoM 
• The tail end of March starts a seasonally bullish period for the KSE-100  
• On Friday 34 shares rose while 63 fell with BAFL PA (+2.96%), ENGROH PA (+0.78%) and AKBL PA 
(+4.55%) were the key upside contributors  
• UBL PA (-2.52%), FFC PA (-0.59%) and OGDC PA (-1.23%) were the biggest index drags 
• China has called on Pakistan and Afghanistan and is working through its own channels to calm the situation 
and follows comments from Pakistan’s Defence Minister Khawaja Asif who declared “open war” with 
Afghanistan 
• The KSE-100 index’s profitability (based on 83% of the index weightage) increased by +5.3% YoY in CY25. 
This earnings increase was witnessed on the back of +50%, +44%, +36%, +31%, +24%, +10%, +10%, +10%, 
+8% and +3% YoY in the Inv. Banking, Auto Assemblers, Miscellaneous, Textile Composite, Cements, 
Banks, OGMCs, Others, Food and Fertilizer respectively.  
• Following a near -15% draw down in the KSE-100 the index is primed for a bounce back towards 175k and 
holding 165k would set up such a move  
• The KSE-100 is currently -5% below the 100-DMA and +7% above the 200-DMA which are long term 
accumulation levels
