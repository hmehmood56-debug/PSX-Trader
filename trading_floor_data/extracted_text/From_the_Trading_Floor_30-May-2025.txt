•

• 
FBR has proposed to increase withholding tax rates on vehicles with an engine capacity above 1300cc in the 
coming budget  
• 
Pharaon Investment Group Ltd.’s potential sale of ACPL PA is attracting initial interest from rivals 
including Cherat Cement Co. and Bestway Group 
• 
Price action remains constructive for higher prices in the coming week against near term support @ 118k
