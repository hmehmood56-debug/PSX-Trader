From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
April 29, 2026 
• Huge day for heavyweight earnings which largely disappointed and saw the market under pressure to lose -
1.54% DoD 
• 21 shares rose while 79 fell with MTL PA (+4.08%), CHCC PA (+2.69%) and DGKC PA (+1.63%) contributing 
the most to index gains  
• UBL PA (-2.65%), NBP PA (-8.77%) and OGDC PA (-3.11%) were the biggest index drags  
• PPL PA (-3.36%) reported 9MFY26 EPS: 22.34, -15% YoY and DPSe: 6.0. PSO PA (-4.37%) reported 
9MFY26 EPSe: 81.19, +150% YoY. NBP PA reported Q1CY26 EPSe: 7.58, -26% YoY. FFC PA reported 
Q1CY26 EPS: 12.14, +30% YoY and DPS: 8.5. DGKC PA reported 9MFY26 EPS: 19.07 +51% YoY. OGDC 
PA reported 9MFY26 EPS: 26.8, -11% YoY 
• Pakistan is preparing to tap international capital markets with a diversified fundraising strategy, as Finance 
Minister Muhammad Aurangzeb said that the issuance of a USD 250mn Panda bond is in the final stages 
• Oil prices are rising again extending a multi-day rally, on reports the US will extend its blockade of Iranian 
ports 
• With the market closed on Friday we are heading into the last session of the week with the KSE-100 -2.9% 
WoW and trading below the previous week’s low from where a pull back into the range is expected
