From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
March 24, 2025 
• 
Selling pressure having hit new all time highs hit the KSE-100 on Monday  
• 
16 shares rose while 79 fell with TRG PA (+2.92%), PABC PA (+5.5%) and ATLH PA (+3.34%) contributing the 
most to index gains  
• 
OGDC PA (-4.04%), PPL PA (-3.59%) and MARI PA (-2.5%) were the biggest index drags  
• 
SYS PA announced CY24 EPS: PKR 25.6, -14% YoY. Earnings in 4QCY24 clocked in at EPS: PKR 6.9, + 32% 
YoY. In addition to the result, the company announced a final cash dividend of PKR 6.00/share and a stock split 
of 5:1, reducing the face value of each share from PKR 10 to PKR 2. As a result, total number of ordinary shares 
will increase from 292.9mn to 1.46bn.  
• 
Looking for the KSE-100 to find support between 115-116k this week
