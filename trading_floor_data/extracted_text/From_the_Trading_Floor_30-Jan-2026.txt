From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
January 30, 2026 
• Recovery on Friday with a +1.01% gain DoD trimmed weekly loses to -2.76% 
• 72 shares rose while 28 fell in the week’s last session with LUCK PA (+3.55%), ENGROH PA (+2.72%) and 
UBL PA (+1.46%) contributing the most to index gains  
• FFC PA (-1.48%), MCB PA (-1.36%) and POL PA (-1.43%) were the biggest index drags  
• Kicking off 2026, headline inflation in the first month of CY26 is expected to settle at +5.8% YoY, marginally 
higher than +5.6% YoY in Dec’25, while remaining well above +2.4% YoY recorded in Jan’25. Core inflation 
(NFNE) for Jan’26 is projected at +7.3% YoY, showing a considerable moderation from +9.0% YoY in Jan’25. 
• SBP’s FX reserves rose by USD 13mn during the week ended January 23, 2026, reaching USD 16.101bn, 
the country's total liquid foreign reserves, including commercial banks' net reserves of USD 5.192bn, stood at 
USD 21.293bn 
• Oil futures jumped to near 6-mth highs on rising concerns about a possible US military attack on Iran which 
remains the key near term risk for equities  
• For the coming week continue to expect the 180k-190k range to remain in play
