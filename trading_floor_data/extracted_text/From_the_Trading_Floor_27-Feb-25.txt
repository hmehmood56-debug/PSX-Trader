From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
February 27, 2025 
• 
Another session with the KSE-100 penned in below 115k 
• 
39 shares rose while 56 fell with OGDC PA (+1.55%), PPL PA (+1.24%), SNGP PA (+5.1%) contributed the 
most to index gains  
• 
LUCK PA (-1.58%), HBL PA (-1.33%) and MEHT PA (-8.42%) were the biggest index drags  
• 
Pakistan’s finance ministry forecasts inflation will slightly increase to 3%-4% in March 
• 
Heading into the last session of the week the KSE-100 is currently +0.87% and our bias remains to the upside 
• 
The most bullish thing the KSE-100 could do would be close out the week above 115k which would require a 
+1% rally
