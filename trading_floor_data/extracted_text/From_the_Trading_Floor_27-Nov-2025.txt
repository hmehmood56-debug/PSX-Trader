From the Trading Floor 
Pakistan  
Stock Market                                                    
November 27, 2025 
• 164k finally breached with the KSE-100 gaining +1.34% DoD  
• 75 shares rose while 21 fell with MEBL PA (+4.42%), LUCK PA (+3.41%) and PPL PA (+3.47%) contributing 
the most to index gains  
• SWCL PA (-0.73%), PIOC PA (-0.52%) and PTC PA (-0.65%) were the biggest index drags  
• Headline inflation for Nov’25 is expected to come in at +6.2% YoY. For 5MFY26, average headline inflation is 
projected to settle around +5.0%, lower than the +7.9% observed during 5MFY25 
• Pakistan and its partners are set to achieve financial close in the next two weeks and secure USD 3.5bn in 
funding for the multibillion-dollar Reko Diq copper and gold mining project 
• The Sindh government has formally approved the strategic partnership submitted by ADM Group China to 
establish more than 600 EV charging stations across the province 
• Heading into the last session of the week the KSE-100 is currently +2.02% WoW and 164k will now need to 
build as support to set up moves back to the Oct highs
